Vision Founder/Chairman’s Desk

Coming together is a beginning; keeping together is progress; working together is success. It is our great pleasure to inform you that xyzschool has come a long way since its establishment and has been able to establish itself as a pioneer school in Nepal. After a long successful academic journey we have come with new spiritual light in education for our academic system.

One of the most important components of a good school is the partnership between the school and parents who work together for the best interest of children. The close partnership between home and school is one of exceptional strengths of xyz School. Steadfast, resolute and determined we all kept together. Day by Day we focused on our goal, keeping together and working together. We faced some incredible and memorable moments that we chose to use as stepping stones as we tenaciously pursued our common goals.

hello

At XYZ School our approach to learning is child centric, encouraging activity based learning and inculating a scientific temperament and a spirit of free enquiry. We motivate our teacher to provide an atmosphere for discovery, where students are encourage to be creative and curious, instead of routing by loading the children with academic burden.

We are thus constantly improving our teaching methodology. So that it turns learning into a combination of classroom study, research and scientific discovery.

It is not only teachers and books which shape the lives of young people, but the space, the environment and the culture prevailing in an institution also influence the sensitive minds. An institution lives not only in its buildings and the comforts that it provides but also in the quality of training that it imparts and the type of students that it produces. Commitment to the best for our children on the part of parents and staff is the key to our success.

We feel immensely proud to say that the dedicated efforts of all the staff members and the hard work put in by the students under the able guidance of our proficient mentors give a new boost to the academic environment and the over-all development year after year. Our insatiable zeal to be innovative and the desire to excel has successfully covered a niche for the school which is today counted among the top school of Nepal.

Our school Prospectus is a true reflection of the efforts and achievements of the staff and students in academics, sports and co-curricular activities.
